# ⚖️ আইন সহায়ক (Ain Sahayak) — Bangladeshi Legal AI

An AI-powered legal advisor chatbot designed to help citizens understand their legal rights in Bangladesh. It covers Family Law, Property Law, Criminal Procedure, and Labor Rights.

### 🚀 [View Live Demo](https://YOUR_USERNAME.github.io/ain-sahayak-ai/)

## ✨ Features
- **Bilingual:** Full support for both Bangla (বাংলা) and English.
- **24/7 Access:** Get legal guidance instantly, no appointment needed.
- **Smart Logic:** Keyword-based response router covering 15+ major legal topics in Bangladesh.
- **Privacy First:** Client conversations are private and local.

## 🛠️ Built With
- Pure HTML5 / CSS3 (No frameworks for maximum speed)
- Vanilla JavaScript
- Google Fonts (Noto Serif Bengali & Playfair Display)

## ⚠️ Disclaimer
This tool provides general legal information based on the laws of Bangladesh. It is **not** a replacement for professional legal advice from a registered advocate.
